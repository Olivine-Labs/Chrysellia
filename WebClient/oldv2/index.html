Neflaria, the land of mystique and magic. Immerse yourself in more than just a game- join wizards and knights of good and evil, all vying for prestige and wealth. Fight your way to the top, or delve into Neflaria's rich history in deep roleplaying experiences. Work together with clanmates and make your clan the most powerful, or instead relax in a casual gaming environment. Whether you're out to be #1, or you're looking for a unique gaming experience, Neflaria builds on a rich 10-year history with a long trail of friends found and lost, connections made, and legendary beasts to kill.

It's easy to get started- create an account and we'll walk you through the character development process. Play from anywhere, using your browser, and forget about installing anything on your computer. Enjoy Neflaria.
